 Welcome to the HCC Engineering Society

 

The HCC Engineering Society is a student government organization for students interested in learning about the various fields of engineering. The purpose of the Engineering Society is to provide a platform for potential engineering students, to inform and broaden their knowledge of engineering topics and fields, and to facilitate peer networking.

For spring 2021, we are meeting on Fridays from 10:30 am - 11:30 PM on our Discord channel. Look for the link below.
